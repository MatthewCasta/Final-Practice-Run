Performing a Brake Job

Before you start, ready the following:

Safety items

-Safety Glasses
-Gloves
-Dirty cloths
-Clean work area

Tools

-Wheel Chocks
-Jack and Jack stands
-Assorted sockets and hand tools

Parts

-Brake Pads and Rotors
-Brake Cleaner Spray
-Rags or Wipes
